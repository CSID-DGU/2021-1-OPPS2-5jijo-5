How to register?

Creating account

You can register with your email address using the costumer code. The code will be provided after the purchase.

Login

Once the registration is confirmed, log in to your account and fill the table section

Charts and Tables

By logging in, you can view the room information in tabular and graphical form.The table page shows registered places, and the chart page shows the temperature and number of people.

Control panel

Various information can be checked in real time as a graph.the temperature of the registeres place can be adjusted autumatically and manually. Here "manual" means that the user can input the desired numerical value.
